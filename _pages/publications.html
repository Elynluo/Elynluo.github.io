23. Rotational ultrasound and photoacoustictomography of the human body
[Yang Zhang*, Shuai Na*, Jonanthan J. Russin*], Karteekeya Sastry, Li Lin, Junfu Zheng, Yilin Luo, Xin Tong, Yujin An, Peng Hu, Konstantin Maslov, Tze-Woei Tan, Charles Y. Liu, Lihong V. Wang
IEEE Transactions On Medical Imaging (2025)
We combine the power of ultrasonography for morphological assessmentof soft tissue with photoacoustic tomography (PAT) for visualizing bloodvessels to enable three-dimensional (3D) panoramic imaging. Specifically,fast panoramic rotational ultrasound tomography and PAT are integratedfor hybrid rotational ultrasound and photoacoustic tomography (RUS-PAT),which obtains 3D ultrasound structural and PAT angiographic images of thehuman body quasi-simultaneously.
